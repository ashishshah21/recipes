Recipie for  1 Cup Tea  : 
3/4th Cup Water
1/4th Cup Milk
1 Tsp Tealeaves
1 Inch Ginger Cube
1 Cardamom 
Sugar to taste

Take Water in a boiling pan and on medium high heat ;
Add Tealeaves
Squash Ginger and Cardamom and add to boiling water;
Take milk in cup and heat in MW for 1 min
Add hot milk to tea mix;
Boil till color is dark brown;
Pour in cup; add sugar ; serve hot (Preferably with Biscuits)
